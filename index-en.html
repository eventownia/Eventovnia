About us

Eventovnia is a group of professional DJs, announcers and instrumentalists

who are trusted by hundreds of customers from Poland and around the world. We conduct parties mainly in Polish and English, but also in German and Spanish. The wealth of 6 years of experience we combine with class and full commitment to each order. Thousands of realized events have resulted in an extremely rich musical repertoire, which thrills to dance and, when necessary, makes the break time more pleasant.

Animations and games, only within the limits of good taste. "What's the tune", Y.M.C.A, integration dances - there is something for everyone. Charismatic hosts, elegant attire suited to the situation, neat stand - this is Eventovnia.

We are at your disposal anywhere on the map. You are welcome to cooperate with us!
